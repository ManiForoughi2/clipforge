ClipForge

Short-form video assembly for Mac.

Pull stock footage, layer voiceover and captions, render in 1080×1920, and send to your TikTok inbox in one flow.

macOS 14 or later · Apple Silicon & Intel

What ClipForge does

🎞️

Stock footage on tap

Search Pexels' library directly from the app and drop licensed clips into your timeline.

✍️

AI-written captions

Generate hook-driven captions and on-screen text with OpenAI — no copywriting required.

🎙️

Voiceover & music

Add text-to-speech narration and pick a music bed from a folder of your own audio.

📤

One-click TikTok upload

Send the rendered MP4 straight to your TikTok inbox as a draft — review and post from the official app.

How it works

1

Connect your TikTok account

Sign in once via TikTok's OAuth flow. ClipForge stores your access token in the macOS Keychain — never on a server we operate.

2

Assemble your clip

Pick stock footage, generate captions, add voiceover and music. ClipForge renders a 1080×1920 vertical MP4.

3

Send to TikTok

Tap "Send to TikTok." The video lands in your TikTok inbox as a draft. Open the official TikTok app to review, edit, or publish.

Privacy, by design

What ClipForge does — and doesn't — do with your data

  • Runs entirely on your Mac. No analytics, no telemetry, no ad SDKs.
  • Your TikTok access token, OpenAI key, and Pexels key live in the macOS Keychain on your device.
  • We don't operate servers that receive your personal data — communication with TikTok, OpenAI, and Pexels happens directly from your Mac.
  • Every upload is an explicit user action. No scheduling, batching, or background posting.
