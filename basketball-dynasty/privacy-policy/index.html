Privacy Policy — Basketball Dynasty: Road to Fame

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

Rutherford Ferrari ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Basketball Dynasty: Road to Fame mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Personal Data

While using our Service, we do not require you to provide personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). However, if you contact us via email for support, we will receive your email address and any other information you choose to provide.

Usage and Diagnostic Data

We collect information on how the Service is accessed and used. This Usage Data may include information such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Other diagnostic data

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability. This data is generally anonymous and does not personally identify you.

Advertising

The iOS version of the Service is ad-free and does not include any advertising SDKs.

The Android version of the Service displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. We do not show banner ads. Only the following ad formats are used:

  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown at natural transition points within the app.
  • Rewarded Ads: Optional ads that the user chooses to watch in exchange for an in-game reward.

To serve these ads, Google AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain information, which may include:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / AAID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
  • General location inferred from IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, completions)

This information is used to serve, measure, and improve advertising, and in some regions to support personalized advertising in accordance with applicable laws.

Consent and Choices (Android)

In regions where required (such as the EEA, UK, and Switzerland), we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to display a consent form before any personalized advertising occurs. You may grant, deny, or change your consent at any time from within the app's settings.

You can reset or limit ad tracking by adjusting your device's advertising settings ("Ads" section in Android Settings) and resetting your Advertising ID.

Purchasing certain in-app products may disable ads entirely.

Google AdMob's privacy practices are described here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

If you make purchases within the Service, the transaction is handled by the Apple App Store / Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your credit card numbers or other financial information. We only receive confirmation from Apple or Google that a purchase was successful to unlock features within the app.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google Firebase (Crashlytics) for crash reporting and app performance monitoring.
  • Google AdMob (Android only) for serving interstitial and rewarded advertisements.
  • Google User Messaging Platform (Android, applicable regions) for collecting and managing advertising consent.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device until you delete the application. We do not store your game data on external servers.

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is designed to be appropriate for users aged 6 and older. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from any user. The app does not require account creation, and stores game data locally on the device. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
